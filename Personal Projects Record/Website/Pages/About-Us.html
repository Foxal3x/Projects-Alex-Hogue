LUCID DREAM ART EXHIBIT

My "Lucid Dream" personal charity digital art exhibition with my mother took place from October 21st to November 2023, marking the debut event for Create With Limits! Through this gallery, we aimed to draw attention to the unique social group of "single-parent families" while also helping children in special circumstances grow up happily and confidently. All the proceeds from this exhibition were donated to the children at the "Shaanxi AiJiaBei Social Care Promotion Center." The sales of the "Hero" comics on-site were also contributed to the funding of Create With Limits' single mother project.

With an attendance of up to 500+ people, we raised a generous amount of $500+, leaving a profoundly positive impact on local children and their families.

One mother shared her daughter's description of the exhibition: "Big brother's artwork is fantastic, and after reading the explanations, I was so moved. I think Mom is the greatest person in the world!" She then hugged me tightly with tears in her eyes!
